Visualization Summary

This visualization shows which countries have the highest life expectancy within a selected continent and year, and how that continent changes over time. The bar chart highlights the top-performing countries in the selected year, while the line chart shows the overall regional trend and allows comparison with a selected country. Overall, life expectancy tends to improve over time in each continent, but the top countries still stand out clearly from the regional average.

Interaction Guide

Use the year slider to explore changes over time and the continent dropdown to focus on a specific region. The bar chart displays the top countries in the selected year, and clicking a country highlights its life expectancy trend in the line chart for comparison with the regional average.
